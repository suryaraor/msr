Why this price?

I'm quaoting this price because of following reasons:

First Owner

I bought this car brand new in 2015, I'm the first and only owner.

There are several reasons why one-owner cars are considered to be the darlings of the used-car world. The primary reason is a consistent maintenance and driving history.

Fewer miles

Used only for commute to work and few long drives.

According to the experts, Low Mileage Matters because miles are a measurable way to quantify a car’s wear and tear. Just like people, cars tend to break down as they get used more and more over time. Lower mileage means components and parts should last longer before failing.

Well maintained

Well maintained, up to date on all services, always did service at Honda service center.

Regular servicing provides peace of mind that the vehicle is in road-worthy condition. Just because a car feels ok, it doesn’t automatically mean all is working as well as it should be.
